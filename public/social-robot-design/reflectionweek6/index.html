Autonomous vs Puppeteered#

Things that have to be autonomous are:

  • Reaction on the user
  • Turn-taking and timing in conversation
  • Navigation and obstacle avoidance

Things that can be puppeteered are:

  • Complex gestures or movements
  • Selecting already made responses
  • Robot asking for help
  • Narrative

Scripted Aspects#

The aspects that can be scripted are:

  • Dialogue
  • Expressions
  • Body language

Performance Criteria#

Design-based performance criteria that can be evaluated are:

  • Timing of responses
  • Amount of errors
  • Trust
  • Engagement
  • Appearance of the robot
